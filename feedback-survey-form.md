# Feedback Survey Form - AgiOnline Platform

## Course Feedback Form

This document outlines the feedback survey form that students complete after finishing their course.

---

### Section 1: Student Information

Students are required to provide:
- **Full Name** (required)
- **Email Address** (required)
- **Phone Number** (required)

---

### Section 2: Course Ratings

Students rate the course using a 5-star rating system:

1. **Overall Course Rating** (1-5 stars)
   - Students select their overall satisfaction with the course

2. **Content Rating** (1-5 stars)
   - Students rate the quality and relevance of the course content

---

### Section 3: Teacher Ratings

For each instructor assigned to the course, students provide:
- **Teacher Name**
- **Rating** (1-5 stars)
- Students can rate multiple teachers if the course has multiple instructors

---

### Section 4: Written Feedback

Students provide written feedback with:
- **Feedback Text** (required, maximum 2000 words)
- Free-form text field where students can share:
  - What they learned
  - What they found most valuable
  - Suggestions for improvement
  - Overall experience and recommendations

---

### Submission Process

1. Students access the feedback form through their dashboard after course completion
2. All fields marked with * are required
3. The form validates:
   - All ratings are between 1-5 stars
   - Feedback text does not exceed 2000 words
   - Student is enrolled in the course
4. Students can update their feedback if needed
5. Once submitted, feedback is stored securely in the database

---

### Data Collection

The feedback form collects:
- Student identification (name, email, phone)
- Course identification (course name and slug)
- Quantitative ratings (overall, content, teacher ratings)
- Qualitative feedback (written comments)
- Timestamp of submission

---

### Privacy and Data Protection

- All feedback data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas
- Access is restricted to authorized administrators
- Data is used for course improvement and quality assurance
- Student information is kept confidential

---

**Note:** This is a digital form accessible through the AgiOnline student dashboard. Students complete it online after finishing their course.
